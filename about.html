about me

Passionate About Animals

Hi my name is Sierra! I’ve been working with dogs for almost 10 years. I have experience doing solo walks/pack walks, boarding/house sitting, day care, training & shelter work (including cats & critters). I’m looking to offer my services locally and get to know the community! I’m insured through Pet Care Insurance (PCI) & can provide references upon request! When your dog is in my care I treat them as my own. They stay in my home with never more than 3 other dogs, get daily exercise (1-2hrs), playtime with toys of their preference and plenty of attention and affection. As well as maintaining/practicing any training you are currently doing with your dog.

I tailor activities to the needs of the dog I am caring for, so an older dog and puppy will have different days. Same with a dog with high energy/drive will have a different day than a couch potato type pup. Either way all their needs will be met and fulfilled
